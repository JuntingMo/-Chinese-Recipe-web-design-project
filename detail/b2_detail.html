Buddha skipping wall

  • Recipe NameBuddha skipping wall
  • Description

    You can eat abalone, sea cucumber, fish lips, Pleurotus eryngii, tendons, mushrooms, cuttlefish, yaozhu, quail eggs

  • LevelHigh
  • Time Needed30min
  • Main IngredientSix dried abalone, one piece of flower glue, six sea cucumbers, one piece of duck, 100g ham, five mushrooms, six shrimps, ten clams, ten quail eggs.
  • Auxiliary Ingredient75g ginger slices, 95g scallion, 10g monosodium glutamate, 75g rock sugar, 75g top soy sauce

  • Process
    1. Blanch the duck legs and pig claws, wash the fresh abalone and dry the sea cucumber. Jinghua ham, abalone, pork, mushrooms, bamboo shoots, pigeon eggs. Fried duck leg and pig claw.

    2. Add other meats, soup, mushrooms and bamboo shoots, and put them at the end. Stew in casserole for 15 minutes.

    3. Collect the juice and serve. Place broccoli and quail eggs around.
