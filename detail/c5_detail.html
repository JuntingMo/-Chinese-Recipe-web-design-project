Dry pot potatoes

  • Recipe NameDry pot potatoes
  • Description

    Dry pot potatoes is a traditional dish in Xiangtan, Hunan Province.

  • LevelHigh
  • Time Needed20min
  • Main IngredientPotatoes, pork belly, bean paste, lettuce, black fungus.
  • Auxiliary IngredientGarlic sprouts, carrots, konjac, green garlic, garlic slices each

  • Process
    1. Cut the potatoes into thin slices, and cut the meat into thin slices.

    2. Start the pan and add oil. Use a little more oil. Add the potato slices and fry them until they are half cooked and set aside for later use.

    3. With the remaining oil in the pot, add ginger and garlic until fragrant, then add the bean paste, add the pork belly and stir the oil until it is fragrant, add the wine, put the light soy sauce, put the sugar, put a small amount of water to boil, put the potatoes and then stir fry.

    4. Lettuce is cut into sections and placed on the bottom of the pan. This is for deliciousness, and the other is to prevent it from sticking to the pan when cooking. Then add the fried meat and sprinkle some garlic leaves and chili on the potatoes to cook.
