Boiled chicken

  • Recipe NameBoiled chicken
  • Description

    The shape is beautiful, the skin is yellow, the meat is white, the fat is tender and delicious, and the taste is very delicious.

  • LevelMiddle
  • Time Needed20min
  • Main IngredientA chicken
  • Auxiliary IngredientTwo chives, a piece of ginger, two star anise, pepper, garlic.

  • Process
    1. Wash the chicken, then soak the chicken in hot water for 15 minutes, and use a small fire.

    2. Wash and cut the onion and ginger, and put them in a small bowl. Add sugar, salt, MSG, vinegar and sesame oil. Mix them well with the fresh soup soaked in chicken.

    3. Then put the chicken into the plate and pour the sauce on the chicken.
