Fish-flavored shredded pork

  • Recipe NameFish-flavored shredded pork
  • Description

    Fish flavored shredded meat has fine ingredients and rich fish flavor. It tastes salty, sweet, sour and spicy, and the texture of shredded meat is soft and tender.

  • LevelLow
  • Time Needed20min
  • Main IngredientPork 300g, a carrot, appropriate amount of auricula, 2 green peppers, shallot
  • Auxiliary Ingredient1 tbsp starch, 1 tbsp cooking wine, 1 tbsp oil, 1 tbsp salt, 3 tbsp sugar and 2 tbsp soy sauce

  • Process
    1. Put two ginger slices in the meat, 1 teaspoon of wine, 1 teaspoon of oil, 1 teaspoon of starch and 1 teaspoon of salt. Then add fish sauce: 4 teaspoons vinegar, 3 teaspoons sugar, 2 teaspoons soy sauce, 1 teaspoon starch. Heat the oil and stir fry the shredded meat until discolored.

    2. Add shredded carrot, agaric and green pepper and stir fry. Pour in the fish sauce and stir well. Put in the scallion section before leaving the pot.
