Spicy Fried chicken

  • Recipe NameSpicy Fired chicken
  • Description

    Spicy chicken is a classic Sichuan dish, generally with the whole chicken as the main ingredient.

  • LevelHigh
  • Time Needed20min
  • Main IngredientChicken leg 500g, dry pepper 40g, hot pepper 5g
  • Auxiliary IngredientAppropriate amount of oil, salt, onion, cooking wine and soy sauce

  • Process
    1. Dry pepper, remove bone from chicken leg, add pepper and cooking wine, marinate for 20 minutes.

    2. When the oil temperature reaches 60% heat, pour in chicken nuggets, pour in soy sauce, and add appropriate amount of salt. Heat the oil to 80% heat, pour in the chicken and fry again.

    3. Add chicken, stir-fry for a while, add a little cooking wine, add chili powder, continue to stir-fry evenly, add Dongan rice vinegar, chicken stock and salt, simmer for 5 minutes before serving.
