East Anglia chicken

  • Recipe NameEast Anglia chicken
  • Description

    A very famous dish in Dong'an.

  • LevelLow
  • Time Needed30min
  • Main Ingredient1 small hen, 50g ginger, 80g chicken fat, 3 millet chili, 1 red chili, a little green onion
  • Auxiliary Ingredient200ml of broth, 150ml of Dongan rice vinegar, appropriate amount of salt

  • Process
    1. After the young hen is cleaned, put the whole chicken in boiling water and cook for 10 minutes. Cut the ginger into shreds and the millet pepper.

    2. Take out the young hen seven mature, cut the chicken into small pieces, then take the pan and cook the oil, add the ginger shreds and the millet pepper, and stir fry.

    3. Add chicken, stir-fry for a while, add a little cooking wine, add chili powder, continue to stir-fry evenly, add Dongan rice vinegar, chicken stock and salt, simmer for 5 minutes before serving.
