Lobster in Chicken Soup

  • Recipe NameLobster in Chicken Soup
  • Description

    This dish is mainly made of lobster, with a seafood dish made of high soup.

  • LevelMiddle
  • Time Needed30min
  • Main IngredientLobster 1.5kg, soup 500g
  • Auxiliary IngredientWater, peanut oil, sesame oil, soy sauce, Shajiang, ginger, onion, coriander, chicken powder

  • Process
    1. Wash the lobster and starch it.

    2. Put the oil in the pan, control the oil temperature at about 180 ℃, fry the lobster until it is seven, and then remove it.

    3. In a new pan, add butter and stock to taste. Finally put lobster in.
