Duck blood in chili sauce

  • Recipe NameDuck blood in chili sauce
  • Description

    It's mainly made of duck blood, and its taste is spicy.

  • LevelHigh
  • Time Needed30min
  • Main IngredientDuck blood (500g), soybean sprouts (150g), tripe (100g), ham sausage (150g), agaric (50g), lettuce (100g)
  • Auxiliary IngredientScallion (50g), salt (3g), pepper (15g), monosodium glutamate (10g), vegetable oil (50g)

  • Process
    1. Pepper, ginger, garlic, salad oil into the pot, stir fry with low heat, add soup after boiling, and then add MSG, sugar, vinegar and other spices.

    2. Cut the main materials, add them into the red soup, bring to a boil, put them into the container, and sprinkle with scallions.

    3. Heat up the salad oil, add the pepper and pour it on it quickly.
