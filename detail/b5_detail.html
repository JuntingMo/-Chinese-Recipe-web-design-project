Creamy fish ball

  • Recipe NameCreamy fish ball
  • Description

    It tastes delicious and can be used as a snack ingredient or even for soup.

  • LevelLow
  • Time Needed20min
  • Main IngredientSpanish mackerel 500g
  • Auxiliary IngredientOnion, starch, egg white, olive oil, salt

  • Process
    1. After the Spanish mackerel is cleaned, scrape off the fish and add appropriate amount of ginger, water and salt.

    2. Put it into the blender and beat it into fish paste.

    3. Put cold water in the pan and bring to a minimum. Then squeeze out the balls one by one and cook them in warm water.

    4. Cook until the fish balls float.
