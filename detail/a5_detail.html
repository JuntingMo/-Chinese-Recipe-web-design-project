Fish filets in hot chili oil

  • Recipe NameFish filets in hot chili oil
  • Description

    "Oil but not greasy, spicy but not dry, hemp but not bitter, smooth and tender meat" is its characteristic.

  • LevelHigh
  • Time Needed20min
  • Main IngredientFish 500g
  • Auxiliary IngredientVegetable oil 150g, pepper 100g, pepper 80g, salt 15g, ginger 20g, garlic 15g.

  • Process
    1. Scrape the fish clean, heat the vegetable oil in the pan, and wait for the oil to heat up.

    2. Add proper amount of mixed oil in the pot and stir the ginger, garlic and watercress until fragrant.

    3. Stir the fish fillets with cooking wine and salt, add water to the pot and bring to a boil. First, boil the fish for 3-5 minutes.

    4. Add the mixed oil in another pot. When the oil pot reaches 7, add pepper successively. When the spicy aroma is strong, pour it on the fish.
