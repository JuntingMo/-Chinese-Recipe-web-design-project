Spicy fired

  • Recipe NameSpicy fired
  • Description

    Spicy fired is a traditional dish in Xiangtan, Hunan Province.

  • LevelMid
  • Time Needed30min
  • Main Ingredient250g chicken breast, 50g water chestnut, 15g pickled pepper, 1 egg
  • Auxiliary Ingredient50g lard, 0.5 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp stock, 0.5 tbsp cooking wine

  • Process
    1. Remove the fascia from the chicken breast, wash and dice, add salt, soy sauce, cooking wine, and monosodium glutamate, mix well and marinate.

    2. Peel the water chestnuts, wash and dice them; remove the stalks of the pickled peppers and chop the seeds; wash the onion, ginger, and garlic and mince; mix the egg white, starch, and water into a thin paste.

    3. Put the wok on high heat, heat up the lard, smash the chicken with a thin paste, and spread it in the pan until cooked.

    4. Add pickled peppers and stir-fry until all the chicken is red with chili, add water chestnuts, ginger, green onions, garlic and stir-fry to get the aroma, and cook the juice mixed with salt, soy sauce, cooking wine, sugar, MSG, starch and stock , Stir fry quickly, add a little vinegar and stir well.
